Commerce in Firearms and Ammunition

This regulation explains requirements for manufacturing, importing, buying, selling, and transporting firearms and ammunition.

Important topics include:

  • Definitions of terms used in this regulation
  • Prohibited shipment, transportation, possession, or receipt of firearms and ammunition by certain persons
  • Armor piercing ammunition (including sporting or industrial purposes)
  • Licensing of manufacturers and importers, collectors, and dealers
  • Administrative actions (license denial, revocation, suspension, or fine)
  • Conduct of business or activity by licensees
  • Importation of firearms and ammunition
  • Records and reports required of licensees
  • Firearms transaction record
  • Acquisition and disposition record
  • Exempt interstate and foreign commerce
  • Relief from firearms prohibitions
