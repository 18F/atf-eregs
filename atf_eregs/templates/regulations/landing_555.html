Commerce in Explosives

This regulation explains requirements for manufacturing, importing, buying, selling, transporting, and storing explosive materials.

Important topics include:

  • Definitions of terms used in this regulation
  • Definition of "explosive materials"
  • Prohibited shipment, transportation, receipt, possession, or distribution
  • License and permit requirements for importers, manufacturers, dealers, and users
  • License and permit denial or revocation
  • Conduct of business by licensees and operations by permittees
  • Records and reports required of licensees and permittees
  • Exemptions to requirements
  • Relief from explosive materials prohibitions
  • Unlawful acts, penalties, seizures, and forfeitures
  • Marking of plastic explosives
  • Storage of explosive materials, including tables of distances
