Importation of Arms, Ammunition and Implements of War

This regulation explains restrictions on importing munitions into the U.S., as a matter that affects world peace and the security and foreign policy of the U.S.

{% endblock %} {% block reg_main_content %}

Important topics include:

  • The United States Munitions Import List (items that are subject to controls)
  • Registration and permit requirements for importing them
  • Import restrictions that apply to specific countries
  • Exemptions to import requirements
  • Penalties for unlawful importation and false statements or concealment of facts; seizures and forfeitures
